The Story Behind American Pie

On February 3, 1959, a tragic plane crash near Clear Lake, Iowa, claimed the lives of rock legends Buddy Holly, Ritchie Valens, and The Big Bopper. Don McLean’s famous song "American Pie" immortalized this moment as "the day the music died." Our site offers an opportunity to explore the history, visit the memorial, and pay respects to these rock ‘n’ roll legends.
